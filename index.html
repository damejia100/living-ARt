Living ARt

Experience art like never before.

Living ARt is an augmented reality (AR) and virtual reality (VR) native mobile app that brings your favorite artwork to life through animations and sound.


About Living ARt

insert youtube video here

Living ARt redefines the way art is experienced by creating an immersive and interactive environment.

The AR experience uses image recognition technology so all you need to do is tap the Open AR button found in app and point the camera at a painting found in our animated library list and an animation will load directly on top of it. Tap and swipe on the playing animation to play a sound description about the piece.

Immerse yourself further using the VR experience. You have the option of choosing regular VR view which displays the animated painting directly in front of you or 360 view which shows the painting all around you. Simply put on your VR headset and watch the painting come to life.
